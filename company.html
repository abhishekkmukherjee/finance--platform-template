About Us

Your Trusted Financial Partner

Founded in 2007, Vedant Asset has grown from a small advisory firm to one of India's leading wealth management companies. Our journey has been built on the foundation of trust, expertise, and client-first approach.

With a team of over 500 financial experts and presence in more than 100 cities across India, we're committed to helping individuals and businesses achieve their financial goals through innovative solutions tailored to their unique needs.

Our core philosophy revolves around creating long-term value for our clients through disciplined investment strategies, comprehensive risk assessment, and personalized service that evolves with their changing needs.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2007

The Beginning

Vedant Asset was founded in Mumbai by Rajiv Mehta with a small team of 5 financial advisors, focusing initially on mutual fund distribution and personal financial planning services.

2011

Expansion Phase

The company expanded to 5 major metropolitan cities across India with a team of 50+ financial experts, and launched its comprehensive wealth management platform offering tailored solutions to HNIs.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched the Vedant Wealth mobile app and online platform, allowing clients to access their portfolios, investment insights, and financial planning tools from anywhere. Assets under management crossed ₹1000 crores.

2019

Product Innovation

Introduced alternative investment products including structured products, PMS services, and international investment opportunities. Established the Vedant Financial Education Foundation to promote financial literacy.

2023

Strategic Growth

Crossed the milestone of serving 500,000+ clients across 100+ cities in India. Launched AI-powered financial planning tools and expanded the corporate advisory division for businesses of all sizes.

2025

Present Day

Today, with ₹5000+ crores in assets under management and a team of 500+ financial experts, Vedant Asset stands as one of India's most trusted wealth management firms, continuing to innovate and expand its services.
